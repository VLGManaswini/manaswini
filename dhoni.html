MAHESH BABU

REAL HERO IN FILM INDUSTRY

☛Prince Mahesh Babu, whose full name is Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu, is a well-known Indian actor in the Telugu film industry.

☛ He's considered one of the most popular and influential actors in South Indian cinema.

☛ Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu was born on August 9, 1975, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He comes from a prominent film family, as his father is the legendary Telugu actor Krishna.

☛Mahesh Babu made his acting debut as a child artist in the film "Needa" (1979) and later made his debut as a lead actor with the film "Raja Kumarudu" in 1999.

☛ He gained widespread recognition and popularity with his performances in various successful films such as "Okkadu," "Pokiri," "Dookudu," "Srimanthudu," "Bharat Ane Nenu," and more.

☛ Mahesh Babu is known for his charming looks, strong screen presence, and ability to portray diverse roles.

☛ He is married to Namrata Shirodkar, a former Miss India and Bollywood actress, and they have two children.

☛ Mahesh Babu is also involved in philanthropic activities and has been associated with various social causes.

☛ One of his prominent initiatives is the "HEAL a Child" program, which focuses on providing medical assistance to children in need.

☛ He has also supported initiatives related to education and healthcare, with a particular emphasis on helping underprivileged children. “REAL HERO”

☛ With financial support from UK based Healing Little Hearts (HLH) and Mahesh Babu, Andhra Hospitals performed surgeries on 1000 children of different ages suffering from a wide spectrum of heart ailments free of cost.
